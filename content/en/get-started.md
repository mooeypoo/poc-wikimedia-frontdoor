# Get started

Welcome to the Wikimedia Developer Portal. This is the starting point for building
with Wikimedia's projects, APIs, and open data — whether you are creating an
on-wiki feature, a bot, a research pipeline, or a commercial integration.

Use the menu on the left to find your path.

## Learn about Wikimedia

- **Wikimedia projects** — Wikipedia, Wikidata, Wikimedia Commons, and more.
- **Wikimedia technology** — the platform, APIs, and data behind the projects.
- **Featured apps** — examples of what the community has already built.

## Build for Wikimedia communities

Extend the wikis themselves: on-wiki features, tools and bots, and MediaWiki
extensions that serve editors and readers directly.

## Build for commercial use cases

Reuse Wikimedia content in your own products — content reuse, embedded learning,
and data to support commercial decision-making.

## Use Wikimedia data in research

Study Wikimedia communities and draw on Wikimedia's open datasets for academic
research.
